# Graham
After Trun and Renald's mushroom trip and spiritual journey together you would know the following about Renald's condition:
- He doesn't fully understand it himself. Why do healing spells still work on him, while he doesn't need to eat, sleep, or breath?
- He can replace almost any body part of his own with someone (or something) else's and it will become functional for him. That is why some parts of his true form look to be from a different person or were mechanical replacements of biological parts.
- He can still feel things he touches, but not in the same way as when he was living. It's like an intangible barrier between him and the living world. Think like trying feeling objects with gloves on. 
- He has no idea how long ago he died, only that it was more than 30 years but less than 40. 
- He died because of a disease called the Creeping Pox. A plaguelike illness that lurks deep under the earth in the Colina and frequently kills miners. It necrotizes the flesh, leaving behind boils and disfigured scarring.
- He remarked that he sometimes feels compulsions that don't feel like his own. Your best guess as to why is as good as his.
- After seeing other walking men of the Colina descend into madness, he ritualistically recites phrases or rhymes his mother used to teach him. This grounds him and reminds him that he is still human, if a little overripe.
