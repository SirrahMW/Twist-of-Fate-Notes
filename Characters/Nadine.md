# Biography
Raised in the upper portion of Colina Pola Plata, Nadine grew up on a ranch with two loving parents who taught her how to care for the land and their animals alike. She learned the basics of animal husbandry and medicine through her experiences as a child in the Plata.

Once she was a young woman, she met an interesting man named Arno, who was traveling through the Plata on his way to the Swamp of all places. It may have been her curiosity about a man that would venture all the way from the Colina to the Swamp for learning about herbs, or it may have been his charm that drew her attention, there is no sure way to know. Maybe it was both.

After a year or two of seeing Arno a handful of times per month, they finally decided to officially try a romantic relationship. Arno had earned enough money to show her a good time by taking her on all sorts of dates, but she quickly became pregnant. This was a big deal for her family because they weren't married yet. So they got married then.

Once their baby boy was born, Arno was already working in the Mines to take care of them. Nadine didn't want the rough life of the Colina to make her son an angry and hateful person like so many. She wanted him like Arno: kind and gentle. She would sing rhymes to [[Renald]] from the time he was a baby, and later would teach him morality through stories that would help shape the man he would eventually turn into.

She also taught Renald how to take care of himself and others, how to wash properly and sanitize for medical procedures, and how to help deliver babies (animal and humanoid alike). Though she had a good grasp of the basics, her medical skills were not enough to save Arno from the Creeping Pox. She could only be with him until he passed.

After that, something in her broke. It was like a piece of her was permanently missing, lost, or altogether changed. She kept up appearances to almost everyone, so only Renald could sense this subtle but important change in her. It got worse though when Renald told her he was going to follow in Arno's footsteps by picking up work in the mines so they could continue living as they had been. 

His mind was already set, so she was unable to sway him. This, combined with the fact that they now saw a lot less of each other put a lot of strain on their relationship. It's no wonder that she cracked the way she did when Ren tried to show her what had happened after his resurrection.
